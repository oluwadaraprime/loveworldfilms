About Us

Spreading The Gospel Through Cinema.

We are the film producing arm of Loveworld dedicated to producing films and audio-visual content that promote the spread of the gospel and inspire the God-culture around the world.

Our Mission

We aim to use the power of storytelling to promote Christian values and spread the message of God's love to people of all backgrounds and beliefs.

Our Vision

Creating inspiring and uplifting films that reflect the beauty and power of faith. We aspire to produce films that touch the hearts and minds of viewers, leaving them with a renewed sense of hope, faith, and purpose.

Our Purpose

To bring glory to God through our work and to be a beacon of light in the world of film and media.
